Nam in Turpis

"Books ... hold within them the gathered wisdom of humanity, the collected knowledge of the world's thinkers, the amusement and excitement built up by the imaginations of brilliant people. Books contain humor, beauty, wit, emotion, thought, and, indeed, all of life. Life without books is empty."
-Isaac Asimov

The Internet is an amazing place, but it's become increasingly commercialized and centralized. The Internet of the 90s and early 2000s was, in many ways, a painful experience. Things were slow, ugly, not color coordinated, flashy, and often a mess. But it was so much easier to find independent content made by regular people. Today, there are all kinds of content that are "small batch" so to speak - some of it wonderfully polished and impressive, some of it is raw in its realness - but it can be hard to find in the dredges of the modern Web. We want to use this space to highlight some of the creative contributions that others have made. Each and every entry here has been hand-selected for one main reason: we think it's awesome.
